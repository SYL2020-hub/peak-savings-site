Privacy is the whole point

Your bank data never leaves your phone.

Most finance apps want your bank login and send your transactions to their servers. Peak Savings does the opposite — your statements are read on the device and the analysis stays there. No account to create, nothing to leak.

Read on-device

Statements are parsed on your iPhone and reconciled to the printed balance — to the cent.

No bank login

You add a statement file — that's it. We never ask for your online-banking password.

Nothing sent out

No cloud, no analytics on your spending, no ads. Delete the app and your data is gone with it.

The app

A clear picture in seconds.

Your total spend, where it goes by category, and the merchants behind it — all read straight from your statements.
